processed">Publicado el 6 de junio de 2013 </span></h3> </div> <p class="body border-bottom pad-ten-bottom">Through
 a top-secret program authorized by federal judges working under the 
Foreign Intelligence Surveillance Act (FISA), the U.S. intelligence 
community can gain access to the servers of nine Internet companies for a
 wide range of digital data. Documents describing the previously 
undisclosed program, obtained by The Washington Post, show the breadth 
of U.S. electronic surveillance capabilities in the wake of a widely 
publicized controversy over warrantless wiretapping of U.S. domestic 
telephone communications in 2005. These slides, annotated by The 
Washington Post, represent a selection from the overall document, and 
certain portions are redacted. <a href="http://www.washingtonpost.com/investigations/us-intelligence-mining-data-from-nine-us-internet-companies-in-broad-secret-program/2013/06/06/3a0c0da8-cebf-11e2-8845-d970ccb04497_story.html">Read related article</a>.</p> <div class="main-content"> <div class="section"> <h4 class="franklin">Presentación del programa</h4> <p class="body">A
 slide briefing analysts at the National Security Agency about the 
program touts its effectiveness and features the logos of the companies 
involved. <!-- <a href="#">Read related article</a>. --></p> <div class="annotated-img"> <img src="NSA%20slides%20explain%20the%20PRISM%20data-collection%20program%20-%20The%20Washington%20Post_files/prism-slide-1.jpg" alt="" class="border"> <div class="pointer small" style="top: 92px; right: 110px;"><span class="small-dot"></span></div> <div class="annotation" style="top: 84px; right: 6px;"><p>El programa es llamado <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/PRISM_%28surveillance_program%29">PRISM</a>, los prismas son usados para dividir la luz, la luz se utiliza para llevar información por medio de cables de fibra óptica.</p></div> <div class="pointer small" style="top: 338px; left: 769px; width: 111px;"><span class="small-dot"></span></div> <div class="annotation" style="top: 330px; right: 6px;"><p>Esta nota indica que el programa es la fuente numero uno de los datos de inteligencia crudos usados para los reportes de análisis de la NSA.</p></div> <div class="pointer small" style="top: 95px; right: 810px; width: 71px;"><span class="small-dot-right"></span></div> <div class="annotation" style="top: 85px; left: 41px;"><p>El sello de la Fuente de Operaciones Especiales (Special Source Operations), muestra la alianza con otras empresas de confianza estadounidenses.</p></div> </div> </div> <div class="section"> <h4 class="franklin">El objetivo es el monitoreo de las comunicaciones</h4> <p class="body">Este diagrama muestra como el volumen de las comunicaciones electrónicas se mueve por medio de compañías con base en los Estados Unidos. <!-- <a href="#">Read related article</a>. --></p> <div class="annotated-img"> <img src="NSA%20slides%20explain%20the%20PRISM%20data-collection%20program%20-%20The%20Washington%20Post_files/prism-slide-2.jpg" alt="" class="border"> </div> </div> <div class="section"> <h4 class="franklin">Los proveedores y los datos</h4> <p class="body">El programa PRISM recoge un amplio rango de datos desde nueve compañías, aunque el detalle es variante de acuerdo con el proveedor.<!-- <a href="#">Read related article</a>. --></p> <div class="annotated-img"> <img src="NSA%20slides%20explain%20the%20PRISM%20data-collection%20program%20-%20The%20Washington%20Post_files/prism-slide-4.jpg" alt="" class="border"> </div> </div> <div class="section"> <h4 class="franklin">Proveedores participantes</h4> <p class="body">This
