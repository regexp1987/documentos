4. Questions about the Fourth Figure

26. With camera BCD we ask: is there any goodness as infinitely great as eternity? And the answer is yes, otherwise the greatness of eternity would not be entirely good.

27. With camera BEF we ask: is God as powerful with his goodness as with his intellect?  Go to this camera and follow the signifiers, correlatives and definitions in it.

28. Question: given that angels are superior to man, can an angel produce another angel, like a man can produce another man, although man is below the angels? the answer is no. An angel receives no increase from outside, or else its essence would be emptied. But a man can produce another man, because he has a body.
