2.Fig."A"

 
2. The Purpose of Figure A.

Since the dignities of A. are per se incessant within themselves, and as they influence creatures with their likenesses, A. enters this Art so creatures may know and love A. while they investigate particular issues and form a universal concept of A. whose propositions are intended as conditions and rules, or exemplary forms for all the other propositions of this Art, for finding particulars in the universal in light of the most holy, essential and intrinsic operation of the divine dignities, and their extrinsic operation through which they influence creatures.
