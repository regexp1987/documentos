QUESTIONS WITH RULE TWO
Questions about CL
1. Question: Who is GOD? Solution: CL, DR.
GOD is the Being in whom infinitely simple extension without any contrariety or composition is identical with eternity; & GOD is the Being who is to be remembered above all beings, & who has greater Concordance and intensity with powers than any other Object.
2. Question: What is the great Concordance between GOD & creature? Solution: CL, EP.
