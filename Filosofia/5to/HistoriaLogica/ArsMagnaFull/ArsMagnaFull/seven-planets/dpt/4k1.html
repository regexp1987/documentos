K. QUESTIONS WITH RULE TEN
Questions about K1L
1. Question: With what does GOD have order in himself? Solution: K1L, BP.
GOD has order in himself with his Glory without Minority, & with the Unity of Goodness, Greatness & Eternity, & with order among the bonifier, bonifiable & bonifying, & with Distinctness between proper & common order, & between power & object; & GOD is ordered with Truth & Equality of the verifier, verifiable & verifying, & the justifier, justifiable & justifying. And thus, in GOD there can be no disorder, punishment, minority, division or separation from Goodness, Greatness & Eternity, & no malice, confusion, contrariety between power & object, falsity, inequality or injury; for none of these things has the wherewithal to exist in GOD.
2. Question: With what did GOD create the world? Solution: K1L, IP.

Questions about K1M
1. Question: With what does GOD glorify the Saints in glory, & with what do they have Glory in GOD? Solution: K1M, FS.
2. Question: With what does man attain the truth about things? Solution: K1M, IS.

Questions about K1N
1. Question: With what does man order memory for remembering, the intellect for understanding & the will for loving? Solution: K1N, ES.
2. Question: With what does the sensitive power amplify its sensing, & the imagination its imagining? Solution: K1N, GL.

Questions about K1O
1. Question: With what does the sensitive power move memory to remember things? And with what does memory move the sensitive power to sense things? And with what does the sensitive power move the vegetative to vegetate? And with what does the vegetative move the elementative power to elementing? Solution: K1O, HS.
2. Question: With what does a power move its object? And with what does man move his feet? And with what does fire move water? And with what is the wind mobile & moved? And with what is the sea mobile? And with what does dew descend? And with what does earth ascend aloft? And with what does fire descend below? Solution: K1O, GS.

Questions about K1Q

2. Question: With what is glorious being made of Glory? And with what does order exist in glory? Solution: K1Q, IS.

Questions about K1R
1. Question: With what are Glory & punishment opposites? Solution: K1R, GS.
2. Question: With what does GOD have extensive Infinity? Solution: K1R, CL.
